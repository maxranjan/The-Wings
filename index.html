About Us

The Wings Prep/Public School is located at Khadi Bhandar Chock, Muzaffarpur, Bihar. It was started on 1 april 2023.Established to provide quality education for students from Class 1 to Class 8, our school is dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning. The aim of this School is to provide an all round formation of students and to impart sound education by inculcating healthy study habits, discipline, self-reliance and moral values. The school also aims at forming students of quality to be leaders in all walks of life and serve their fellow human beings in Justice, Truth and Love.
